EMERGENCY CARE

We offer 24/7 emergency care services
with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced medical professionals.

Mission

“To provide immediate and standby services to all patients in critical conditions, mostly accident and injusry or bullet patients.
We aim to save those at the risk of losing lives due to blood loss and injuries”

Goals

  • Immediate response to all patients in critical conditions
  • Life saving services to all patients
  • Surgery and operation services

Objectives

  • Response to all patients in critical conditions
  • Create a safe environment for bloody patients
  • No death due to blood loss and injuries

Why Emergency Unit as a Clinic

Uganda loses 318 people everyday due to accidents and injuries. Most of these die due to limited attention at the hospitals, limited blood banks and financial issues

Our emergency clinic is here to save lives and provide immediate care to all patients in critical conditions. We assume evrything to be a priority and we do not leave any patient behind

After assuring th safety of any live, we then charge you with the least amount affordable to the care takers, relatives or anyother summaritan willing to borrow a helping hand

We treat everyone, with no segregation, no racism for base for any gender or any age. We are Godly clinic with efforts to reduce the number to 10 people by 2055
